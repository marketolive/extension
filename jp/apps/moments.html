Marketo Moments
Marketo Moments is a mobile app that makes it easy for marketers to keep their fingers on the pulse of their marketing efforts, anywhere, anytime.
With Moments, Marketers will be able to:
  • (1) See what is important the moment you open the app
  • (2) See how recent campaigns are performing
  • (3) Take actions on upcoming campaigns
  • (4) Share with others
  • (5) Measure Success
